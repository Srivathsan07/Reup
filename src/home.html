When will my box be delivered?

We will contact you about scheduling your box delivery at a convenient time when you will be home to receive it. Due to cannabis laws, your box must be handed to you upon delivery.

Do I need a medical card to sign-up?

No! You just need to be 21+ years of age.

Can I pick the products in the box?

We have you take a quick survey so that we can curate monthly boxes to your preferences while also introducing new brands and items. Currently the boxes are not customizable for individual items but you can purchase directly through our partners.
